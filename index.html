About Red Bull

Our Story

Red Bull is a leading energy drink company known for its innovative marketing and sponsorship of extreme sports and events. Founded in 1987, Red Bull has since become a global brand recognized for its involvement in sports, culture, and entertainment.

Events and Sponsorships

Red Bull hosts and sponsors a wide range of events, from high-energy sports competitions like Red Bull Rampage to music festivals and art showcases. The company's commitment to adrenaline-fueled activities has made it a favorite among thrill-seekers and sports enthusiasts.

Community Engagement

Beyond its products, Red Bull actively engages with communities through initiatives that promote creativity, athleticism, and sustainability. The Red Bull Wings for Life World Run, for instance, raises funds for spinal cord research.
